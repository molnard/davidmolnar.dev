## What it is

A 3D printed fridge magnet commissioned by GingerWallet as a promotional item for Bitcoin Prague 2026. Designed from scratch, prototyped, then produced in a run of 100 units. Modeled in Fusion 360, sliced and printed on a Bambu Lab printer.

## The design

The brief was open: create something that communicates CoinJoin visually. The result is an angular, circuit-board style magnet that maps the privacy transaction directly onto the physical object.

- Matte black base plate
- Central raised GingerWallet logo
- Bitcoin coin inputs on the left, identical outputs on the right
- Angular gold transaction traces connecting them
- Large `COINJOIN` label
- Smaller `gingerwallet.io` text
- Green leaf accent

The layout is not decorative — it's a diagram of how CoinJoin works.

## From concept to production

1. Received the original GingerWallet STL reference and brand assets
2. Multiple design directions explored, circuit-board style selected
3. Full parametric model built in Fusion 360
4. Prototype prints evaluated
5. Bambu Studio production plate laid out for batch printing
6. 100 units manufactured for Bitcoin Prague 2026
